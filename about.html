01 : About Me

Biography

My name is Bethany Marie Colgrove. I'm a multidisciplinary artist based in San Diego, California. I am familiar with various forms of media, but UI design has become a passion of mine. I used a free website template and my limited skills in HTML and CSS to redevelop and redesign this website you are currently on. Consider this website an educational project example. This is my first completed project. The second completed project is my Wix portfolio site.

Education

I am due to complete my education at Cal State San Marcos with a Bachelor's Degree in Art, Media, and Design in 2023. My education has helped me gain art skills for traditional and digital media, UI included. I have also completed additional web-related courses from Aquent Gymnasium. I am currently earning my UX design certificate. I am simultaneously taking a self-directed appraoch to learning Figma.

Goals

My multidisciplinary education has given me the ability to craft aesthetically-pleasing, functional, and effective design. I also specialize in accessible UI design.
